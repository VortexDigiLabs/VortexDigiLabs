# Brand Design DNA: Vortex Digi Labs

## 1. Core Aesthetic & Vibe
*   **Theme:** Cyberpunk, High-Tech, Futuristic, E-sports, Premium Digital Agency.
*   **Mood:** Cutting-edge, dynamic, high-energy, and bold.
*   **Visual Style:** Dark mode dominant with high-contrast neon illumination. It relies heavily on 3D metallic chrome textures, optical lens flares, and subtle geometric/circuit-panel background patterns.

## 2. Color Palette (Approximate Hex Codes)
*   **Deep Space Background:** `#0A0A0C` to `#16161A` (Very dark charcoal/almost black). Acts as the dark canvas to make the neon lights pop.
*   **Neon Cyan (Primary Glow):** `#00E5FF` to `#00B3FF`. Used for under-glows, rim lighting, tech accents, and cool-toned highlights.
*   **Energy Red (Secondary Glow/Action Color):** `#FF2A2A` to `#E60000`. Used for aggressive contrast, background panel lighting, and emphasizing key text (e.g., "INNOVATION & CREATIVITY").
*   **Chrome & Steel (Logo/Neutral):** `#FFFFFF` (Pure White) mixing with `#A0AAB5` (Cool Silver) and `#4B5563` (Dark Metal). Used to create the reflective, beveled 3D look of the main logo.

## 3. Typography Guidelines
*   **Logo/Hero Display Type ("VORTEX DIGI LABS"):** Highly stylized, sharp, angular, sci-fi/e-sports display font. It features distinct serif-like spikes, diagonal cuts, and a heavy 3D metallic bevel.
*   **Tagline/Supporting Text ("FUTURE OF DIGITAL..."):** A modern, ultra-bold, clean, geometric sans-serif font. It grounds the design and ensures readability.

## 4. UI & Composition Elements (The "DNA")
*   **Lighting Effects:** Heavy use of bright, horizontal lens flares (light leaks) to give a cinematic, high-budget feel.
*   **Edge Treatments:** The logo text features distinct glowing strokes/outlines offset from the main letters (cyan on top/left, red on bottom/right) to simulate holographic dispersion or neon backlighting.
*   **Background Textures:** Not completely flat black. The background features matte, dark grey armor-like plating or sci-fi tech panels with sharp 45-degree angle cuts and subtle red/blue glowing crevices.
*   **Shape Language:** Aggressive, forward-leaning, and sharp. Avoid soft, highly rounded corners in favor of polygons and parallelograms.
